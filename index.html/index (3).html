Goblin Barrel

A well-balanced Logbait control deck with reliable defense and multiple win conditions. It is competitive up to mid to high ladder if played smart.

Strengths

Two Win Conditions:

You can punish with Goblin Barrel and also pressure with Skeleton Barrel — both bait out small spells.

Rocket Finisher:

Perfect for tower chip + punishing big elixir plays (like Pump, Wizard, or Evo Witch near tower).

Defense Core:

Valkyrie + Cannon + Skeletons gives excellent ground coverage. Dart Goblin adds strong ranged pressure.

Fast Cycle:

Average elixir ≈ 3.0, which means you can cycle back to Goblin Barrel quickly even if it is countered.

⚠ Weaknesses

No Strong Air Tank Killer:

Lava Hound or Balloon decks can be tricky — Cannon + Dart Goblin can hold, but you’ll need precise timing.

Predictable Bait:

If your opponent saves Log/Barrel perfectly, you might struggle to break through — vary your Goblin Barrel placement (corner, front, deep).

Squishy Defenders:

Dart Goblin and Skeletons die easily to spells, so you need good spacing. Overall, this is a solid bait deck and works against most common matches, and i will give it a solid rating of 9.
